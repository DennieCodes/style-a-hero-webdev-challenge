Our curriculum

Our program will take your photography skills to a professional level over the course of an intense six week program that ranges from mastering exposure to developing an attractive and marketable portfolio.

Week 1

Master Exposure

Use aperture, shutter speed and ISO to master exposure in any and all shooting situations

Week 2

Ambient and Supplemental Lighting

Use natural and artificial lighting to create dramatic contrasts and shadows in your photos

Week 3-4

Composition

Learn to position your camera or subject to create visually interesting and engaging photos

Week 5-6

Portfolio Development and Promotion

Showcase the best photographs in your portfolio and promote your talents
